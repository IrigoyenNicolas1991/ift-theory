## Honest scoreboard (July 2026)

- **Calibrated: 2 numbers** (β=4, Eddington 1919 eclipse; its temporal placement, Mercury).
- **Reproduced: 10 observables** (E=mc², Newton, γmc², redshift/GPS, light deflection,
  Shapiro delay, non-dispersion, perihelion, Nordtvedt, geodetic precession).
- **Own falsifiable predictions:** black-hole shadows **+4.6%** / ringdown **−4.4%** vs GR,
  anticorrelated — ngEHT and 3rd-generation detectors decide.
- **Falsified (by us, published same day):** the elastic-linear radiation sector
  (LIGO measures spin-2; an isotropic elastic medium gives spin-0 ⊕ spin-1 —
  [nota 7](notas/NOTA-antenas-falsacion-2026-07-02.md)); the linear galactic regime
  (5× off at low acceleration vs SPARC — [nota 8](notas/NOTA-galaxias-RAR-2026-07-02.md)).
- **Open reconstruction ("TCI 2.0", speculative, clearly flagged):** tensor-order condensate
  substrate — see [notas 9–11](notas/) and [`especulativo/`](especulativo/) (branch
  `desarrollo-fable`), which carries its own warning sign.

Every numerical claim ships with a verification script that first reproduces the exact GR
values as a control. Found an error? [Open an issue](../../issues) — a confirmed external
falsification gets published here with credit, same protocol as our own.
